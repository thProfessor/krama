# Pitch Deck: Krama
## *Bringing Order to Inventory with the Power of Voice*

---

## 1. The Problem
**Inventory management is slow, manual, and error-prone.**
- Warehouse and retail staff often have their hands full, making it difficult to stop and type data into a system.
- Manual data entry leads to "inventory drift" (mismatches between system records and physical reality).
- Complex UI dashboards make searching for specific items a tedious task.

---

## 2. The Solution
**A Hands-Free, Multi-Lingual Intelligence Layer for your Inventory.**
- **Voice-Activated Transactions**: "In" and "Out" movements logged by speaking in English, Hindi, or local dialects.
- **"Bharat-Ready" Intelligence**: Advanced support for Hinglish and code-switching (e.g., *"Aaj 50kg steel out kiya"*).
- **Automated Visuals**: AI matches inventory items with images automatically.
- **Instant Insights**: A real-time dashboard accessible to everyone, regardless of language barrier.

---

## 3. Core Features

### 🎙️ Multi-lingual Voice Engine
Users can say: 
- *"Added 15kg of Copper Wire"* (English)
- *"15 kilo tamba jama kiya"* (Hindi)
- *"15kg steel nikaal lo"* (Hinglish)
The backend adjusts stock accurately across all inputs.

### 📊 Real-time Dashboard & Alerts
- Visual bar charts and stock-level indicators.
- Proactive notifications: "Item X is below 10% – Reorder now!" 
- Stock-out alerts sent directly to mobile or email.

### 🔍 Global AI Search
Fast, indexed search across the entire inventory database. Find any item by name, category, or voice query.

### 🖼️ Visual Cataloging
- Automatic image matching based on item descriptions.
- High-quality image uploads to ensure warehouse staff pick the right items.

---

## 4. Target Market
1.  **Small & Medium Warehouses**: Need efficiency without the cost of heavy industrial ERPs.
2.  **Retail Store Backrooms**: Fast-paced environments where hands-free entry is a game-changer.
3.  **Production Workshops**: Tracking raw materials (metal, wood, fabric) by weight or length.
4.  **Hobbyists/Personal Collectors**: Managing parts, components, or collections with ease.

---

## 5. Competitive Advantage
- **Frictionless Entry**: Voice reduces data-entry time by up to 80% compared to typing.
- **Low Barrier to Entry**: No expensive hardware required (runs on smartphones/tablets).
- **AI-Driven Accuracy**: Natural Language Processing (NLP) eliminates "fat-finger" errors.

---

## 6. Business Model (Proposed)
- **SaaS Subscription**: Tiered pricing based on the number of items or users.
- **Enterprise Customization**: Custom AI training for specific industry terminologies or hardware integrations.

---

## 7. The Vision
To become the "Operating System" for physical goods, where the gap between physical stock and digital record is closed by the simplest human interface: **Speech.**
